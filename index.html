About me

Programming student currently studying at Karelia University of Applied Sciences in Joensuu Finland, looking forward to working as a front-end web-developer.

Having worked assembly jobs at various factories throughout my twenties, in 2020 I decided to venture into the world of web-design and programming.

In my freetime I've always enjoyed drawing, and incorporating those skills into my career as a web-developer will definitely come useful.

During summer, I enjoy motorbikes! They go fast 🏍

My strengths at the moment lie in HTML, CSS and little in JavaScript, but during the first half of 2022 I intend to expand my knowledge more into frameworks/libraries such as React.

While I am mostly interested in the front-end lifecycle at the moment, in the future I would like to dip my hands in the world of the back-end. To help guide me with this path, I am currently taking the Full Stack Open online course provided by the University of Helsinki. This course is mainly done in my freetime alongside the rest of my education, but I should have the course done by the end of may.
